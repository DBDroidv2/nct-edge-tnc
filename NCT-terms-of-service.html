A guide to understanding and using Edge coins on the NCT platform.

What are Edge coins?

Edge coins are a specially generated token that are awarded to users for making purchases on the NCT platform. Edge coins can be used to redeem discounts, rewards, and other benefits on the platform. Edge coins are not a cryptocurrency and do not have any monetary value outside the platform.

How to earn Edge coins?

Users can earn Edge coins by shopping on the NCT platform and paying with any of the supported payment methods. Users will receive 10% of the order invoice value as Edge coins in their NCT wallet. For example, if a user spends INR 1000 on the platform, they will receive 100 Edge coins in their wallet. The Edge coins will be credited to the user's wallet within 24 hours of the order confirmation.

How to use Edge coins?

Users can use Edge coins to redeem discounts, rewards, and other benefits on the NCT platform. Users can view the available offers and benefits on the Edge coins page on the platform. Users can also check their Edge coins balance and transaction history on their NCT wallet page. To use Edge coins, users need to select the Edge coins option at the checkout page. The equivalent amount of the order value will be deducted from the user's Edge coins balance. For example, if a user has 200 Edge coins in their wallet and wants to buy a product worth INR 500, they can use 100 Edge coins to get an INR 100 discount and pay the remaining INR 400 with any of the supported payment methods.
