EdgeCoin Policy and Terms of Use

The following outlines our policy and terms of use for EdgeCoin.

What is EdgeCoin?

EdgeCoin is a digital token coin that is awarded to users for shopping on the website and referring new users to the platform. The cashback on this platform is given to users in the form of EdgeCoin.

Earn EdgeCoin by Shopping

Activity Commission
As a user you can earn EdgeCoin by shopping on the website 10% of your purchase amount
You can earn a maximum of 100,000 EdgeCoin per day Not Applicable

Referring New Users to the Platform

Activity Commission
