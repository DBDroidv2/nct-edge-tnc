Privacy Policy

NCT Enterprises ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and security when using our website at nctenterprises.in. This policy will help you understand how we collect, use, and share your information when you visit or make a purchase from our website.

What information do we collect?

When you visit the website, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device. Additionally, when you make a purchase or attempt to make a purchase through the website, we collect certain information from you, including your name, email address, and billing address. We also collect information you provide when you fill out a form or otherwise communicate with us.

What do we use your information for?

We use the information we collect from you when you visit, or make a purchase from, our website for the following purposes:

  • To process your transactions, including sending order confirmations, processing payments, and providing you with invoices and other documents related to your transactions.
  • To communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, including for customer service, marketing, or administrative purposes.
  • To send you information or statements about your account with us, and give you access to our website and services.
  • To display personalized content, including advertisements, offer promotions, and suggest products or services based on your interests.

How do we protect your information?

We take precautions to protect your information. When we collect information from you through the website, we secure it with appropriate security measures, such as using Secure Sockets Layer/Transport Layer Security (SSL/TLS) encryption. We also Limit access to your information to the workers who need it to perform their jobs, and who have agreed to keep the information confidential. We may also use security measures, such as firewalls and encryption, to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Do we share your information with third parties?

We may share your information with third parties to help us use your information as described above. For example, we may use your information to:

  • Fulfill and manage your orders, including sending you order confirmations and tracking information.
  • Communicate with you about your use of our website and services, including by email, social media, or on our website.
  • Use your information to show you ads or content, based on your interests, on our website, other websites, or on other platforms.
  • Send you marketing and promotional communications by email, text message, or postcard.
  • Send you administrative information, such as invoices, statements, and other notifications related to your account.
  • Display your personal information in the "Reviews" sections of our website, so other users can see your name and headline when you leave a review.
  • If applicable, use your information to provide you with information about products or services offered by our partners that may be of interest to you.
If we have your consent, we may share your information with third parties for their specific purposes. If you provide us with information about someone else, their information may be shared with third parties for their specific purposes.

How long do we keep your information?

We keep your information for as long as we need to. In some cases, this will be less than a year, and in other cases it will be more than a decade. For example, we will keep your information as long as you are a registered user of our website, or as long as we are required by law to keep it.

What are your options?

If you have an account with us, you may choose to delete it at any time. If you wish to delete your account, you can follow the instructions in the "Account Settings" section of our website. If you have access to a mobile device, you may be able to uninstall the app that provides access to our website. If you are a registered user of our website and have deleted your account, we will not be able to reactivate it and your information will not be retrievable.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to the law, changes to the way we do business, or changes to our practices. We will let you know when we make significant changes to our privacy policy by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your account, unless you have specified not to receive these notices. If we need to make changes to this privacy policy to comply with applicable law, we will try to provide notice through our normal legal process. When we make changes to our privacy policy, the updated policy will be posted on this page, and the date at the top of the page will be updated.

We may also provide notice to you in other ways, such as by email, if we have a legitimate reason for doing so.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or if you have questions about how we handle user data, you can contact us by email at customercare@nctenterprises.in.
