Automatic Information

Examples of the information we collect and analyze include:

  • the internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the internet;
  • login, e-mail address, and password;
  • the location of your device or computer;
  • content interaction information, such as content downloads, streams, and playback details, including duration and number of simultaneous streams and downloads, and network details for streaming and download quality, including information about your internet service provider;
  • device metrics such as when a device is in use, application usage, connectivity data, and any errors or event failures;
  • NCT Enterprises metrics (e.g., the occurrences of technical errors, your interactions with service features and content, your settings preferences and backup information, location of your device running an application, information about uploaded images and files such as the file name, dates, times and location of your images);

  • purchase and content use history, which we sometimes aggregate with similar information from other customers to create features like Nctenterprises Bestsellers;

Examples of information we receive from other sources include:

Information You Can Access

Information You Can Access

You can access information about your account, orders, and interactions with our website, mobile application, and other services on the NCT Enterprises website by following these steps:

  1. Log in to your account on the NCT Enterprises website.
  2. Click on your name in the top right corner of the page.
  3. Click on "Account" or "Orders" to view information about your account, orders, and interactions with our website, mobile application, and other services.

We also provide you with the ability to delete your account and certain types of data, including:

  • Order history; and
  • Certain information about your interactions with our website, mobile application, and other services, including search queries and webpage activity.

To delete your account, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account on the NCT Enterprises website.
  2. Click on your name in the top right corner of the page.
  3. Click on "Account" and then click on "Delete account".
  4. Follow the instructions to confirm that you want to delete your account.
